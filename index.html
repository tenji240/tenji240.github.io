The Domus Project

The Domus Project is the creative realization spawned by a love for the arts. It started early with music, and from there moved on to film and written work.

The goal of The Domus Project is to celebrate various mediums of entertainment, ask interesting questions about game design, and provide solid analysis and reviews, in order to become a consistent voice of criticism in the video game space.
